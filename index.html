Nestled in the heart of Bengaluru, Ignyt is not just a beer shop; it's a sanctuary for those who cherish the golden brew. Born from a passion for craft beer and a dream to create a unique social experience, Ignyt stands as a beacon for beer enthusiasts and social butterflies alike.

  • At Ignyt, we celebrate the diversity of beer with a selection that spans the globe.
  • From the hoppiest IPAs to the smoothest stouts.
  • Our meticulously curated collection is a testament to our love for beer's rich narratives and diverse flavor profiles.

Join us for a journey through the world of craft beer, where every sip tells a story, and every visit is an opportunity to make new memories. Welcome to Ignyt - where every moment is a celebration of beer, community, and the endless joys of life.
